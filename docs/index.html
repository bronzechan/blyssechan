Blysse Chan

Story to raise her from toddler to a kiddo

This is the family of Blysse. We want to raise her in a warm family and drive her to challenge new knowledge with confidence.

  • Get Started

Enjoy being a young kid

Being a kid in Kindergarten should be the happiest part of her life. No Pressure, Stress, or over-education. We believe having fun is the key to love learning - we make sure she enjoys expressing her creativity

  • Learn More

Give her chance to show love

Kid learns through mirroring our behaviors. We totally realize how true it is. Soon we realize she would do exactly what we do to love us back. Pick up camera and shoot pictures for us, saying good morning everyday, and even crouch down to talk to us (come on! I can't get further lower anymore)

  • Learn More

Capability over Certification

We have the luxury to be with her at home most the time. That gives us opportunities to teach her on our own. We are able to train her for skiing, swimming, ice-skating, roller skating, scootering, sewing, fashion-designs and building legos. She is in love with all the activities.
