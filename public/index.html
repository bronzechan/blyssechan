What's special about her

To us - she is the best gift for our family. Lovely, gentle, caring and full of laughter.
We are so proud of her progress while still always get shocked with her ability to catch new skills.
Here are some of the highlights.

Bi-lingual

Blysse speaks very well with both English and Cantonese. With two languages in mind, it gives her multiple ways to express her thoughts with precise vocabulary.

Underwater

Blysse is learning to swim with her family. She can now hold her breath for 12 seconds and have fun gliding underwater for treasure hunt.

Ski

She learned skiing with in 2 days and grasped the body control very well. She can enjoy riding the chairlift and skiing on green slopes confidently.

Comment by teacher #1

Blysse has been consistent this semester in displaying good habits in the classroom. She is an active learner who enjoys participating and engaging in class discussions.

Comment by teacher #2

Her writing skills have become stronger over the semester and her attention to detail in her work has made very good improvement.

Comment by teacher#3

Blysse's ability to understand and interpret texts has made significant progress. She has a strong command of vocabulary in her speech and continues to expand on her vocabulary range on a daily.

Creative
